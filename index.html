CHICBLOCKO CUSTOMS

How To Buy Your Own Custom

Follow these steps to start your custom order.

  1. 01

    Join the Discord server

    Join the official Chicblocko Discord server.

    JOIN DISCORD
  2. 02

    Open a Purchasing ticket

    Create a ticket in the Purchasing channel.

    CREATE TICKET
  3. 03

    Ask for custom pricing

    Custom prices range from $150 to $400.

  4. 04

    Discuss your custom

    You will then be added to a group chat with the owners to discuss your custom or submit your finished model file.

  5. 05

    Wait for the owner

    Wait for the owner to confirm that your custom is finished. You will receive it after the server resets.

Custom pricing depends on the requested design and complexity.
