Privacy Policy

Chicblocko Gun Values is a community-made static website. It does not use accounts, ads, or tracking cookies. Editor data stays in your browser unless you export it.

Information Collected

The public website does not use accounts, payment forms, analytics scripts, or tracking cookies. The custom editor can save edits in your current browser only, using local browser storage such as IndexedDB.

Local Browser Data

Editor changes, imported backups, and image previews stay on the device and browser where they were created unless you export and share them yourself.

Third-Party Assets

Some community record images may load from third-party image hosts. Those hosts may receive normal browser request information when the page displays those images.
